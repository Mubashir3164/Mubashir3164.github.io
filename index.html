About Me

I am Mubashir Abbas, a PhD graduate in Molecular Biology and Biochemistry, and currently a Postdoctoral Fellow. My research expertise spans computational and experimental biology.

Key Expertise:

  • NGS Data Analysis: Variant calling, genome assembly, transcriptomics
  • Population Genetics: Studying genetic diversity and population dynamics
  • Functional Genomics: Root systems and plant stress responses
  • Bioinformatics Tools: Linux, Python, R for high-throughput data analysis
